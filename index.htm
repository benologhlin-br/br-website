Why Your Back Pain Keeps Returning

Most people try stretching, massage, strengthening exercises, or forcing themselves to “sit up straight.”

But the tension patterns causing the pain often remain unchanged.

Alexander Technique helps you become aware of unconscious habits that may be creating unnecessary strain in your:

  • Neck
  • Back
  • Shoulders
  • Breathing
  • Sitting and walking patterns

This Isn’t About Forcing Perfect Posture

Instead of “correcting” your body, we focus on helping you:

  • Release unnecessary tension
  • Improve coordination
  • Move more efficiently
  • Reduce compression and strain
  • Develop sustainable body awareness

Many clients notice easier sitting, standing, walking, and less tension throughout the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Alexander Technique?

Alexander Technique is a movement education method that helps improve awareness, coordination, posture, and tension habits.

Is this physical therapy?

No. It’s an educational approach focused on movement awareness and habit change.

Do I need prior experience?

No experience is necessary.

How many sessions do people usually do?

Many clients begin with an introductory session and continue with a short series for lasting changes.

Is this appropriate for chronic pain?

Many people with chronic tension and posture-related discomfort find it beneficial, though results vary by individual.
