Back pain doesn't usually need fixing.
It needs less tension.

Most back pain comes from the tension you build and hold throughout the day — often without noticing.

Alexander Technique lessons help you notice and reduce this tension in how you sit, stand, and move — so your back pain is often less intense and less disruptive in daily life. A free 30-minute intro lesson is the best way to explore if it's right for you.

What Alexander Technique lessons focus on

Recognising and reducing unnecessary muscular tension in everyday activities like sitting, standing, walking, and working. The work is not about stretching or strengthening, but changing habitual patterns of effort in real time.

Clinical research (ATEAM study, BMJ)

A large UK randomized controlled trial found that Alexander Technique lessons were associated with meaningful improvements in chronic back pain and quality of life compared with usual care.

People experience a range of outcomes — from improved comfort and reduced daily pain to, in some cases, complete resolution of long-standing symptoms.
